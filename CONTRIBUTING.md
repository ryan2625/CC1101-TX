# Contributing to **ESP32-CC1101-TRANSMISSON**

Thank you very much for taking the time to contribute! All feedback and ideas are greatly appreciated.

## About Contributing
1. **Questions are welcome.**  
Open an issue with a detailed title and description for your question. The author will get back to you as soon as possible.
2. **Improvements.**  
See something that is misleading, needs refinement, or is straight-up incorrect? Please open a PR with your changes, and leave a comment with an explanation about why this change should be implemented. Cite sources such as authoritative documentation, the datasheet, etc when applicable. Alternatively, leave an issue and refer to the specific information you believe should be changed and why. The author will review this on a case-by-case basis.

## AI Use

**This guide was written by a human with minimal AI assistance.** Any proposed changes & contributions to this repository must be led by a human. 

Thanks!!!
